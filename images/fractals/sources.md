## Concepts Recommended for AI Generation

The following concepts may benefit from AI image generation due to limited public domain options or to achieve specific visual aesthetics for toddlers:

1. **Infinity** - Abstract visual representations of infinity are rare in public domain; AI can create colorful, child-friendly infinite tunnel/mirror effects

2. **Tessellation (Escher-style)** - M.C. Escher's work is copyrighted; AI can generate original animal/shape tessellations inspired by the style

3. **Mandelbrot Close-ups** - While public domain options exist, AI can generate custom color palettes and zoom levels optimized for toddler visual development

4. **Abstract Vortex** - Most public domain vortex images are realistic water/air; AI can create stylized, colorful spiral vortices
