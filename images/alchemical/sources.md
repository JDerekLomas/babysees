## Key Historical Sources

### Michael Maier - Atalanta Fugiens (1617-1618)
- **Wikimedia Category:** [Category:Atalanta_Fugiens](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Atalanta_Fugiens)
- **Files:** 300+ images
- **Description:** Alchemical emblem book with 50 emblems, each combining image, epigram, and musical fugue. Engraved by Matthias Merian.
- **Notable Emblems:** Labyrinth, ouroboros/dragon, philosophical imagery

### Heinrich Khunrath - Amphitheatrum Sapientiae Aeternae (1595)
- **Wikimedia Page:** [Amphitheatrum sapientiae aeternae](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Amphitheatrum_sapientiae_aeternae)
- **Key Image:** [Alchemist's Laboratory](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Alchemist%27s_Laboratory,_Heinrich_Khunrath,_Amphitheatrum_sapientiae_aeternae,_1595.jpg)
- **Description:** Circular elaborate engravings heightened with gold and silver, designed by Khunrath and engraved by Paullus van der Doort.

### Robert Fludd - Utriusque Cosmi Historia (1617-1621)
- **Wikimedia Category:** [Category:Robert_Fludd](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Robert_Fludd)
- **Related Category:** [Category:Macrocosm and microcosm](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Macrocosm_and_microcosm)
- **Description:** 60+ intricate engravings exploring the Microcosm (human life) and Macrocosm (universe/Divine). Famous title page shows human form overlying planetary spheres.

### Basil Valentine - Azoth (1613/1659)
- **Wikimedia Category:** [Category:Azoth (Basil Valentine)](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Azoth_(Basil_Valentine))
- **Files:** 16 images
- **Key Image:** Rebis/Androgyne standing on dragon
- **Description:** Alchemical illustrations including the famous Azoth mandala showing stages of transformation.

### Splendor Solis (1532-1582)
- **Wikimedia Category:** [Category:Splendor Solis (1582) - BL Harley MS 3469](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Splendor_Solis_(1582)_-_BL_Harley_MS_3469)
- **Parent Category:** [Category:Splendor Solis](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Splendor_Solis) (40 files)
- **Description:** The most beautiful illuminated alchemical manuscript in the world. 22 full-page miniatures from British Library Harley MS 3469.

### Rosarium Philosophorum (c. 1550)
- **Wikimedia Category:** [Category:Rosarium philosophorum](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Rosarium_philosophorum)
- **Files:** 31 images
- **Key Image:** Der grune Lowe (The Green Lion devouring the Sun)
- **Description:** 20 famous woodcut illustrations depicting alchemical processes and the coniunctio (union of opposites).
