### Stained Glass

**Chartres Cathedral Rose Window**
- **Category**: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Rose_windows_of_Cath%C3%A9drale_Notre-Dame_de_Chartres
- **Description**: North Rose Window (ca. 1220), known as "Rose of France," featuring the Virgin and Child surrounded by prophets
- **Artist**: Unknown medieval glaziers
- **Source**: Wikimedia Commons
- **License**: Public Domain (artwork) / CC BY-SA (photographs)
- **Notes**: The stained glass itself dates to 1145-1240 and is public domain; photographs have various licenses

**Tree of Jesse Window**
- **Location**: West facade, Chartres Cathedral
- **Date**: ca. 1150-1170
- **Description**: One of the oldest surviving stained glass windows, depicting Christ's genealogy

### Tapestry

**Bayeux Tapestry**
- **Digital Panorama**: https://www.bayeuxmuseum.com/en/the-bayeux-tapestry/discover-the-bayeux-tapestry/explore-online/
- **Description**: 70-meter embroidered cloth depicting the Norman Conquest of England (1066)
- **Date**: ca. 1070 (within years of Battle of Hastings)
- **Medium**: Wool embroidery on linen
- **Source**: Bayeux Museum digital collection / Wikimedia Commons

## Primary Source Repositories

1. **Metropolitan Museum Open Access**: https://www.metmuseum.org/art/collection/search?showOnly=openAccess
   - CC0 license for 490,000+ images
   - API available for bulk access

2. **Wikimedia Commons**: https://commons.wikimedia.org/
   - Largest repository of free media
   - Various CC licenses, check each file

3. **Rijksmuseum Open Access**: https://www.rijksmuseum.nl/en/rijksstudio
   - 709,000+ images, CC0/Public Domain
   - Medieval and Renaissance works

4. **Unsplash**: https://unsplash.com/
   - Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required
   - Architecture and travel photography

5. **Pexels**: https://www.pexels.com/
   - Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required
   - Architecture and interiors

6. **Rawpixel Public Domain**: https://www.rawpixel.com/
   - CC0 licensed images from museums
   - High resolution downloads
